1. What we collect

  • Account data — your name (or business name), email address, role, and password (stored only as a salted hash, never in plain text). If you sign in with Google, we receive your verified email and name.
  • Listing and booking data — details you enter about spaces, services, bookings, and any photographs you upload.
  • Payment data — handled by our payment partner. We receive a payment reference and status, not your card or bank numbers.
  • Product analytics — we log events such as searches and bookings to understand and improve the marketplace. This uses a first-party, random visitor identifier only. We do not collect IP addresses, device fingerprints, or third-party tracking identifiers.

2. Why we use it (purpose)

  • To create and secure your account and provide the booking, payment and messaging features.
  • To connect advertisers with owners and suppliers and to process confirmed bookings and commission.
  • To provide customer support and send you service-related communications, including password-reset emails.
  • To understand demand and improve pricing guidance and the product. Aggregated, non-identifying insights may inform which cities and formats we develop.

3. Legal basis and consent

We process your data on the basis of the consent you give when you create your account and accept this policy, and to perform the services you request. You may withdraw consent by closing your account, subject to records we must retain by law.

4. Sharing

We share data only as needed to run the service: with the counterparty to a booking (an owner sees the advertiser's booking details and vice-versa), with our payment and email service providers, and where required by law. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, and meet legal, tax and accounting obligations. Transaction records are retained as required by applicable law.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed with a per-user salt. Sessions use HttpOnly cookies. Access to production data is restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

7. Your rights

Under the DPDP Act you may, subject to its conditions, request access to, correction of, or erasure of your personal data, and nominate another person to exercise your rights. To make a request, contact our Grievance Officer at bookmyboard.help@gmail.com. If you are not satisfied, you may escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.

8. Children

BookMyBoard is not intended for anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. When we make material changes, we will ask you to accept the updated version the next time you use the platform.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: bookmyboard.help@gmail.com.
