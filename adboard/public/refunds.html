Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

How cancellations and refunds work on BookMyBoard. This policy forms part of our Terms of Service.

1. Before a booking is confirmed

You pay only after a space owner approves your request. Until then, a booking request carries no charge, and you may withdraw it at any time at no cost.

2. If the owner declines

If an owner declines your request, no amount is charged. If a payment was made and the owner is unable to honour the booking, you receive a full refund.

3. Cancelling a paid booking

Because outdoor advertising space is reserved for specific dates and taken off the market once booked, cancellation terms depend on timing relative to the campaign start date:

  • 7 or more days before start: refund of the amount paid, less the platform commission and any payment-gateway charge already incurred.
  • Less than 7 days before start: refunds are at the owner's discretion, since the dates can no longer be resold. We will help mediate.
  • On or after the campaign has started: not eligible for refund.
These windows are our default. An individual owner may offer more flexible terms on their listing; where they do, the more generous terms apply.

4. If the campaign is not delivered as described

If your creative is not put up, is materially different from the confirmed listing, or proof of display is not provided when promised, contact us. Where we determine the space owner did not deliver, you are eligible for a full refund.

5. How refunds are issued

Approved refunds are made to the original payment method through our payment partner. Once initiated, refunds typically reach your account within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank. The platform commission is non-refundable except where the refund is due to non-delivery or owner cancellation.

6. How to request a refund or cancellation

Email hello@bookmyboard.in with your booking reference and reason. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. When we make material changes, we will ask you to accept the updated version the next time you use the platform.
